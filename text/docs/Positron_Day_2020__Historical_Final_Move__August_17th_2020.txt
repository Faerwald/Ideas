Positron Day 2020: Historical Final Move, August 17th 2020
Faster than light, not in translation speed (which was a normal walking pace) but in knowledge I had
yet learned, I left my house, "teleporting" through my back woods, until I reached my first destination:
The “Heartbreak Streak" adjacent to the "Innovators Terminus" that was my second destination. There
I sat, "Thinking FTL". I reached out, grabbed the grass with my hands, and threw it upwards:
     “Augusta Ada Lovelace.”
I spoke faster-than-light. I will never give up. I love Augusta Ada Lovelace, the real original woman.
